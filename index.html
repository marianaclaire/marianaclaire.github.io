I was born seeing auras and entities. At the age of 3, I remember speaking with my guides and other entities. At the age of 5, I blocked this ability when I was introduced to the school system like most of us do. I was raised in Lima, Peru and from a young age, I expressed myself through drawing, singing, and writing. I have always loved to help, advise, and heal people. It wasn't until later in life that I realized I was doing healings and readings from a very young age without even knowing I was doing them.

About 2 years ago, I became more aware of my abilities when I made a conscious decision to start developing them. The critical shift began when I met my spiritual teachers: Pamela Aaralyn, Teal Swan and Dwaine Hartman. I remembered who I was and my purpose in this life. This is the first life that I have decided to live humbly and unlock my gifts one by one. This was so that I could experience being fully human in order to reach out to others and to understand them better.

In terms of academics, I am currently majoring in Psychology and this has allowed me to understand people at a deeper level.

So far I have unlocked my channeling and healing abilities, clairsentience, clairvoyance, clairaudience, clairscent and clairempathy. I am currently working on unlocking even more gifts but I know that patience and flow are my main purposes in this life so they will come one by one.

During the 2015 shift, I had another awakening experience that inspired me to learn reiki and become a vegan. Most importantly, I stepped into the public eye to share my abilities with others.

My goal right now is to help people to remember who they are and to awaken to their personal power. I believe that we can all do this if we do the work and that that it's actually the reason we incarnated at this time. So let's do this!
